Our intelligence engines operate at the intersection of mathematics, chemistry, and physics — purpose-built for industrial asset complexity. The full technical documentation is available to qualified partners under NDA.

Engine 01 · Mathematics
Stochastic Optimisation
Multi-objective industrial routing

Multi-objective solvers for asset routing, waste logistics, and offtake scheduling across complex industrial networks. Designed to handle the combinatorial complexity of NCS decommissioning sequences and agricultural supply chains simultaneously.

Asset Routing Waste Logistics Multi-Objective Optimisation Offtake Scheduling
Applied to NCS decommissioning planning and agricultural waste collection logistics — reducing routing overhead and increasing circular material yield.
Engine 02 · Physics
Physics-Informed ML
Conservation-law-constrained models

Machine learning models constrained by physical conservation laws — ensuring outputs are physically plausible, not just statistically correlated. Applied to well integrity simulation and industrial bioprocess control where unconstrained models produce commercially dangerous predictions.

Well Integrity Simulation Bioprocess Control Conservation Constraints Drift Monitoring
Validated against publicly available NCS reference datasets. Results are reproducible and auditable — a prerequisite for regulatory and operator acceptance.
Engine 03 · Chemistry
Reaction Kinetics Engine
First-principles biological modelling

First-principles fermentation and degradation modelling for biological assets and organic waste streams. Predicts conversion yields, quality outcomes, and carbon credit eligibility before a litre of substrate is committed — reducing capital risk in bioprocess investment decisions.

Fermentation Modelling Yield Prediction Degradation Kinetics Carbon Eligibility
Developed under the NFR Seafood Industry research programme — institutional validation of scientific rigour and commercial relevance.
Engine 04 · Statistics
Hybrid Temporal Modelling
Real-time anomaly and state detection

Advanced sequential modelling for real-time anomaly detection and state-change classification in continuous industrial data streams. Combines probabilistic frameworks with deep learning for early warning of asset degradation, process deviation, and emissions exceedance — with explainable outputs for operator action.

Anomaly Detection State Classification Emissions Monitoring Early Warning
Subject to academic peer review. Methodology details available to research and institutional partners on request.
